Background

I grew up in a small town in Utah by the name of Delta. It's a thriving metropolis of about 3500 people. Most people don't think there's a lot to do out there, 100 miles from the nearest real city, but I always managed to find plenty to do to keep busy. I left for an LDS mission to Texas and then school at BYU where I met my wife. We've been in the Provo-Orem area since.

Family

I have been married for 18 years and have 5 children. My wife is from Georgia and we met while going to school. Our children range in age from 14 to 3. We have lived in south Orem for the last 8 years.
